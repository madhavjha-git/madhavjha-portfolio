About Me

I’m Madhav Jha, software engineer by profession, a bad guitar player and someone who loves mountains. Originally from Bihar,I grew up in Kolkata and currently based in Pune.

As a kid, I wanted to be a painter, then a cricketer and finally I got introduced to computers at my uncle's place and ever since I just wanted to be a "Computer Engineer". I find my thrill in building something and seeing it work.

I'm a passionate, detailed-oriented, responsible, and committed professional, with a get-it-done, on-time, and high-quality product spirit. I have close to 7 years of experience defining requirements, designing, implementing, testing, and delivering complex back-end applications using a variety of programming languages and technologies.

Apart from work, I have been helping my friends and relatives go digital with their business. This gives me a chance to experiment and help people at the same time.
